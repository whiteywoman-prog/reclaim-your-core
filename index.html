Pre & Post Workout Nutrition

Target: 15–20g protein + 30–40g carbs

Why: Never lift fasted — cortisol is already elevated in the morning, and menopause amplifies this. Pre-workout fuel blunts the cortisol spike and provides energy.

  • Cottage cheese + banana
  • Smoked salmon + toast
  • Protein shake + oatmeal
  • Cottage cheese + berries

Target: 40g protein (higher than standard 20–25g)

Why: Anabolic resistance in postmenopausal women means muscle protein synthesis requires a higher protein dose to activate. Aim for 2.3–3.5g leucine per meal.

Leucine-rich sources are critical for optimal muscle protein synthesis.

  • Protein shake + chicken breast
  • Salmon + quinoa
  • Cottage cheese + protein powder

Protein: 30–40g + Carbs: 40–60g

Glycogen replenishment is key after endurance work. Combine protein with carbs for recovery.

  • Smoothie with protein + banana + oats
  • Turkey sandwich + fruit
  • Recovery shake + granola bar
  • 4–5 meals per day, each with 25–40g protein — anabolic resistance means each meal needs a higher dose
  • Leucine-rich proteins: whey, chicken, fish, beef, collagen peptides
  • Omega-3 rich foods: salmon, sardines, walnuts — anti-inflammatory
  • Cortisol-lowering foods: dark leafy greens, avocado, dark chocolate, fermented foods, chamomile tea

Program Reference

Monday — Upper Body Push

  • Warm-up: Foam roll thoracic spine + lats (2 min), band pull-aparts 2×15, arm circles + shoulder CARs
  • Barbell Bench Press: 4×4–5 @ 80–85% 1RM
  • Barbell Overhead Press: 4×4–5 @ 80–85% 1RM
  • Incline DB Press: 3×8–10
  • Cable Lateral Raises: 3×10–12
  • Tricep Dips: 3×8–10
  • Finisher: Plank hold 3×30–45s

Tuesday — Lower Body (Quad/Glute)

  • Warm-up: Foam roll quads, adductors, glutes (3 min), bodyweight squats 2×10, banded glute bridges 2×15
  • Barbell Back Squat: 4×4–5 @ 80–85% 1RM
  • Front Squat or Goblet Squat: 3×6–8
  • Bulgarian Split Squat (DB): 3×8–10 each
  • Leg Press: 3×10–12
  • Hip Thrust (Barbell): 3×8–10
  • Finisher: Pallof Press 3×10 each side

Wednesday — Active Recovery

  • Vagus Nerve: Cold water face splash (30s), deep breathing 4-7-8 (3 min), humming/chanting (2–3 min)
  • Planks: 3×30–60s
  • Pallof Press: 3×10 each
  • Bird Dogs: 3×10 each
  • Fascia Release: Foam roll IT band + thoracic spine (5 min), lacrosse ball glutes + plantar fascia (4 min)
  • Cool Down: Gentle yoga — cat-cow, child's pose, thread the needle (5 min)

Thursday — Upper Body Pull

  • Warm-up: Foam roll lats + pecs (2 min), band pull-aparts 2×15, scapular wall slides 2×10
  • Barbell Bent-Over Row: 4×4–5 @ 80–85% 1RM
  • Weighted Pull-ups or Lat Pulldown: 4×4–6
  • Seated Cable Row: 3×8–10
  • Face Pulls: 3×12–15
  • Barbell or DB Bicep Curls: 3×8–10
  • Finisher: Dead Bug 3×8 each side

Friday — Lower Body (Hinge/Posterior)

  • Warm-up: Foam roll hamstrings, calves, piriformis (3 min), hip 90/90 mobility 2×5 each, banded monster walks 2×12 each
  • Barbell Deadlift: 4×3–5 @ 80–90% 1RM
  • Romanian Deadlift: 3×6–8
  • Single-Leg RDL (DB): 3×8–10 each
  • Glute-Ham Raise or Nordic Curl: 3×6–8
  • Standing Calf Raises: 3×12–15
  • Finisher: Hanging Leg Raises 3×8–10

Saturday / Sunday — Cycling

  • Cycling ride (duration varies)
  • Light stretching and fascia work
  • Focus on pre/post ride nutrition
  • Protein target: 1.6g/kg bodyweight (105g for ~64kg)
  • Meal timing: 4–5 meals/day, 25–40g protein each
  • Pre/post workout nutrition: See the Nutrition tab for detailed guides
  • Hydration: 2.5–3L water daily
  • Distribute protein evenly across meals — anabolic resistance means each meal needs a higher dose to trigger muscle protein synthesis
  • Magnesium glycinate: 200–400mg before bed (sleep + cortisol)
  • Vitamin D3: 2000–4000 IU daily (bone density + mood)
  • Omega-3: 2–3g EPA/DHA daily (anti-inflammatory)
  • Creatine: 3–5g daily (muscle, brain, bone density benefits for postmenopausal women)
  • Ashwagandha: 300–600mg daily, KSM-66 extract (cortisol reduction)
  • Collagen peptides: 10–15g daily (joint + skin support)

Vagus Nerve Stimulation

  • Cold water splash (face/wrists) — 30 seconds
  • Humming/chanting — 2–3 minutes (vibration stimulates vagus nerve)
  • Deep slow breathing: 4-7-8 pattern (inhale 4, hold 7, exhale 8)

Fascia Release

  • Foam rolling: IT band, thoracic spine, hip flexors — 5 min
  • Lacrosse ball: glutes, plantar fascia — 2 min each
  • Gentle yoga: cat-cow, child's pose, thread the needle — 5 min
  • Morning: 10-min walk (cortisol regulation via sunlight)
  • Sleep: 7–9 hours, cool room, no screens 1hr before
  • Stress: Journaling, meditation, or creative activity
  • Community: Social connections reduce cortisol
  • Take measurements every Sunday morning
  • Progress photos monthly
  • Deload every 4th week (reduce weight by 40%, keep volume)
  • Review and adjust protein target monthly
